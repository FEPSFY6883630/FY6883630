The Role of Wearable Technology in Sports

Exploring Innovation, Injury Prevention, and Performance Enhancement

Presented by Moeed Dewan, (6883630) ENG0018

Abstract

Wearable technology revolutionizes sports by providing real-time analytical insights into performance and injury prevention. This project explores its mechanisms, benefits, and challenges, highlighting its transformative role in modern sports.

Article

The full article explores how wearable technology aids in performance monitoring, injury prevention, and recovery optimization. It also delves into ethical concerns such as data privacy and accessibility challenges faced by non-professionals.
